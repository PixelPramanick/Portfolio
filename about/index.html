SHILPI CHAUHAN CHARABORTY

Shilpi Chauhan Chakraborty is an Independent Visual Communication Designer practising design since 2007. She is a Post Graduate in Graphic Design from National Institute of Design (NID), Ahmedabad and currently works from Kolkata, India.

She takes keen interest in academia and believes it is her duty to contribute to her fraternity. Shilpi has given talks at NIFT, Kolkata and Sister Nivedita University, Kolkata. She also mentors students at NSHM Design School.

Shilpi is also the President of Association of Designers of India (ADI) - Kolkata Chapter (2023-2025)

Her intense belief in developing meaningful and memorable designs creates timeless communication solutions for her clients. Besides grey matter, she invests her heart & soul in each project!

Specialties: Logo Design, Branding, Editorials, Environmental Graphics and Print Ads.
